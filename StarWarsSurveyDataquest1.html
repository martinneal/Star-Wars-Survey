Empire is the highest ranked Star Wars movie in the survey, with Return and New Hope coming in 2nd and 3rd respectably. All three prequel movies rank also 0.5 points higher that the three orginial movies.

Would like to dig deeper into the age of the participates, & impact only seeing a few movies would impact the rankings. I hypothesize that the older participates are more likely to rank the original movies higher than the new movies. While there is also a chance that some participates may have only viewed the prequel movies.
